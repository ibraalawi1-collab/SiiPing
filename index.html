Privacy Policy for SiiPing

Last Updated: November 27, 2025

Introduction

SiiPing ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Username, email address, password (encrypted)
  • Profile Data: Profile picture, status message, PIN code
  • Communication Data: Messages, images, voice recordings, and other content you share

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, interaction patterns, session duration
  • Location Data: We do NOT collect precise location data

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the SiiPing service
  • Enable communication between users
  • Improve user experience and app functionality
  • Send notifications about app activity
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

  • End-to-End Encryption: Messages can be encrypted end-to-end
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored on Supabase servers with industry-standard security
  • Data Retention: Messages and content are retained as per user settings (temporary messages auto-delete)

Data Sharing

We DO NOT sell your personal information. We may share data only:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • With service providers (Supabase, Google Sign-In) who assist in app operations

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection
  • Export your data

Children's Privacy

SiiPing is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Third-Party Services

SiiPing integrates with:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • Google Sign-In: Optional authentication method

Please review their privacy policies separately.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.
